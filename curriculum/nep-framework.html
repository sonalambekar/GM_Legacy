Curriculum Framework

Our Curriculum Framework

At GM Legacy School, the curriculum is designed in accordance with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, with the aim of providing a holistic, flexible, and learner-centric education from the foundational years to the secondary stage.

Our curriculum framework integrates academic learning, skills, values, arts, sports, and technology, preparing students to meet the challenges of higher education, careers, and responsible citizenship.

Principles Guiding Our Curriculum

The NEP-based curriculum at GM Legacy School is built on the following core principles:

Holistic Development

Focus on cognitive, emotional, physical, social, and ethical development.

Competency-Based Learning

Emphasis on understanding, application, critical thinking, and problem-solving rather than rote memorisation.

Experiential Learning

Learning through activities, projects, experiments, field visits, and real-life contexts.

Flexibility and Choice

Multiple pathways and subject choices, especially at the secondary stage.

Integration of Skills and Values

Life skills, vocational skills, and value education are integrated across subjects.

Inclusivity and Equity

Equal opportunities and appropriate support for all learners.

Our Commitment

At GM Legacy School, the NEP-based curriculum framework ensures that learning is meaningful, joyful, and relevant. Students develop strong foundations and future-ready skills while education is rooted in Indian values and global outlook.

Through this curriculum, we aim to nurture confident learners, ethical leaders, and lifelong learners.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
