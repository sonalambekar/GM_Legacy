Teaching and Learning at GM Legacy School

Teaching and Learning at GM Legacy School

At GM Legacy School, teaching and learning are guided by the belief that every child can learn and succeed when provided with the right environment, guidance, and opportunities.

Our teaching–learning processes are designed to make education meaningful, joyful, engaging, and effective, while preparing students for academic excellence, life skills, and responsible citizenship.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Our teaching philosophy is based on the following principles:

• Learner-Centred Education – The child is at the centre of the learning process.

• Active Learning – Students learn best by doing, questioning, and reflecting.

• Holistic Development – Equal importance to academics, skills, values, arts, and sports.

• Inclusivity – Every learner is supported according to individual needs.

• Continuous Improvement – Teaching practices are reviewed and refined regularly.

Teaching–Learning Approaches

1. Concept-Based Teaching

• Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorising facts.

• Use of examples, demonstrations, and real-life applications.

2. Experiential and Activity-Based Learning

• Learning through experiments, projects, role play, and field visits.

• Hands-on activities in classrooms and laboratories.

3. Inquiry and Problem-Based Learning

• Encouraging students to ask questions, investigate, and solve problems.

• Development of critical thinking and analytical skills.

4. Differentiated Instruction

• Teaching strategies adapted to suit different learning styles and abilities.

• Remedial support for slow learners and enrichment for advanced learners.

5. Integration of Technology

• Use of smart classrooms, digital resources, and online platforms.

• Promoting digital literacy and responsible use of technology.

Role of the Teacher

At GM Legacy School, teachers are:

• Facilitators of learning, not just transmitters of information

• Mentors and guides who nurture curiosity and confidence

• Role models who uphold values, discipline, and integrity

• Lifelong learners who continuously update their knowledge and skills

Regular teacher training and professional development programmes ensure high-quality teaching.

Learning Environment

We provide:

• Well-designed, child-friendly classrooms

• Science and computer laboratories

• Library and reading spaces

• Activity rooms and sports facilities

• Safe, clean, and inclusive campus

The learning environment is designed to be stimulating, safe, and supportive.

Assessment Integrated with Teaching

• Continuous and formative assessments guide teaching strategies.

• Regular feedback helps students understand their strengths and areas for improvement.

• Teaching is adjusted based on assessment outcomes.

Student Support Systems

• Academic mentoring and counselling

• Remedial classes and enrichment programmes

• Career guidance and life skills mentoring

• Support for students with special educational needs

Parental Partnership

We believe in a strong school–parent partnership. The school:

• Communicates regularly with parents

• Conducts Parent–Teacher Meetings

• Shares academic progress and feedback

• Encourages parental involvement in school life

Learning Outcomes

Through our teaching–learning processes, students will:

• Develop strong conceptual understanding

• Become independent and confident learners

• Think critically and creatively

• Communicate effectively and collaborate well

• Grow into responsible, ethical, and capable individuals

Our Commitment

At GM Legacy School, we are committed to:

• High-quality teaching and learning

• Continuous improvement and innovation

• Child-centred and value-based education

• Preparing students for academic success and life beyond school

We strive to make every classroom a place where learning is joyful, meaningful, and transformative.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
