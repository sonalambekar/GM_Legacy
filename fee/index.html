Payment Schedule

The school follows a structured and transparent payment schedule.

• Fees may be payable in one annual instalment or in two to four term-wise instalments, as notified.

Typical instalment schedule:

• First Instalment: At the time of admission / June

• Second Instalment: September

• Third Instalment: December

• Fourth Instalment (if applicable): February

• Due dates will be communicated through circulars and the school portal.

• Late payment may attract late fees as per school rules.

Mode of Payment

Fees can be paid through the following modes:

Online Payment

School Portal / Payment Gateway

UPI / Net Banking

Quick and secure digital payments

Demand Draft

In favour of GM Legacy School

Cash / Card

At School Office (where permitted)

Receipts are issued for all payments, and parents are advised to retain them carefully.

Fee Rules & Refund Policy

• Fees once paid are non-transferable.

• Admission fee and registration fee are non-refundable.

• In case of withdrawal before the commencement of the academic session, refund will be processed as per school refund rules.

• No refund is admissible after the academic session has commenced, except in special cases approved by Management.

• Fee revision, if any, will be notified in advance.

Transport Fee

• Transport fee is separate from school fee.

• Fee depends on the distance and route of the student.

• Transport fee is payable on a term-wise or annual basis.

• Transport facility is optional and subject to availability of seats in buses.

• Details of route-wise transport fees will be provided at the time of admission.

Hostel Fee (If Applicable)

For students availing residential facilities:

Hostel fee generally includes:

• Boarding and lodging

• Food and dining charges

• Electricity and water

• Medical and supervision charges

• Basic laundry and housekeeping

Hostel fee is payable term-wise or annually, as notified separately.

Scholarships & Concessions

GM Legacy School may offer:

• Merit-based scholarships for academically outstanding students

• Need-based concessions in deserving cases

• Concessions for siblings (as per policy)

• Special scholarships as approved by the Management

All scholarships and concessions are granted strictly as per school policy and are reviewed annually.

Fee FAQs

1. Is there any donation or capitation fee?
No. The school strictly follows no-donation, no-capitation policy.
2. Can fees be paid in instalments?
Yes. Fees may be paid in term-wise instalments as notified.
3. What happens if fees are paid late?
Late payment may attract a late fee and may lead to withholding of report cards or hall tickets.
4. Are books, uniform, and transport included in school fee?
No. These are charged separately.
5. Is fee revision done every year?
Fee revision, if any, is done only after approval of Management and as per government norms.
6. Can I change transport route during the year?
Route changes are subject to availability and approval.
