Vision, Mission & Objectives

Vision

To build a world-class school that nurtures curious minds, strong character, and capable citizens, and to grow into a leading educational institution serving learners across Karnataka and neighbouring states by 2035.

Mission

  • To provide joyful, inclusive, and high-quality education from the foundational to the secondary stage.
  • To implement the principles of NEP 2020 through holistic, skill-based, and value-driven learning.
  • To create a safe and inspiring environment where every child can discover their potential.
  • To prepare students for higher education, meaningful careers, and responsible citizenship.

Objectives

Academic Objectives

  • To offer a strong NEP-aligned curriculum across Foundational, Preparatory, Middle, and Secondary stages.
  • To develop foundational literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
  • To integrate academics with arts, sports, technology, and life skills.

Student Development Objectives

  • To promote moral values, citizenship, and social responsibility.
  • To support physical, emotional, and mental well-being of every child.
  • To identify and nurture individual talents and interests.

Institutional Objectives

  • To grow steadily from a small beginning in 2026 to a multi-campus, high-quality institution by 2035.
  • To attract students from across Karnataka and neighbouring states through quality and trust.
  • To build a team of committed, well-trained, and ethical educators.
  • To maintain high standards of governance, safety, and accountability.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
