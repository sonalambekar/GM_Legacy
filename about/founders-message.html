Founder’s Message

It gives me immense happiness and gratitude to welcome you to GM Legacy School.

My journey in education began over two decades ago with a simple belief — that quality education has the power to transform lives, families, and society. From the establishment of GM Institute of Technology in 2001, to GM First Grade College, GM Institute of Pharmacy and Research, GM Halamma Pre-University College, and most recently GM University in 2023, every institution under the GM banner has been built with a single purpose: to serve society through education.

The decision to start GM Legacy School is a natural extension of this journey.

I strongly believe that the foundation of a nation is laid in its schools. The early years of a child’s life shape not only academic abilities, but also character, values, discipline, and compassion. With GM Legacy School, our aim is to create a nurturing environment where children learn with joy, grow with confidence, and develop into responsible and capable citizens.

We begin this school with humility, but with a clear long-term vision. By 2035, we aspire to grow into a leading institution serving students from across Karnataka and neighbouring states, while always preserving our core commitment to quality, integrity, and child-centric education.

I express my sincere gratitude to parents, teachers, staff, and well-wishers who place their trust in us. Together, let us build not just a school, but a legacy of learning that will guide generations to come.

With warm regards,

Sri. GM Lingaraju

Secretary

Srishyla Education Trust, Davanagere

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
