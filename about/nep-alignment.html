NEP 2020 Alignment

Our Academic Vision

At GM Legacy School, our academic vision and practices are closely aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, which aims to transform school education into a more holistic, flexible, multidisciplinary, and learner-centric system.

We believe that NEP 2020 provides a strong framework to prepare children not only for examinations, but for life, work, and responsible citizenship in the 21st century.

NEP-Based School Structure

In accordance with NEP 2020, GM Legacy School follows the 5+3+3+4 curricular structure:

Foundational Stage (5 Years)

Early childhood education focusing on play-based learning, language development, numeracy, motor skills, and socio-emotional growth.

Preparatory Stage (3 Years)

Building strong foundations in reading, writing, mathematics, environmental studies, arts, and physical education through activity-based learning.

Middle Stage (3 Years)

Introducing subject-based learning with emphasis on critical thinking, scientific temper, problem-solving, and experiential learning.

Secondary Stage (4 Years)

Providing multidisciplinary education, flexible subject choices, skill courses, and preparation for higher education and careers.

Key NEP 2020 Practices

1. Holistic Development

We focus on cognitive, emotional, physical, social, and ethical development of every child.

2. Competency-Based Education

Teaching and assessment emphasise understanding, application, and skills rather than rote memorisation.

3. Experiential Learning

Learning is enriched through projects, experiments, field visits, arts, sports, and hands-on activities.

4. Foundational Literacy

Strong emphasis on early reading, writing, and numeracy skills in the foundational and preparatory stages.

5. Flexible Learning

Students are encouraged to explore multiple disciplines including sciences, humanities, arts, sports, and vocational skills.

6. Assessment for Learning

Continuous and formative assessments guide learning and support improvement, rather than only ranking students.

7. Integration of Technology

Digital tools are used to enhance teaching, personalised learning, and teacher development.

8. Teacher Development

Continuous training and capacity building of teachers as mandated by NEP 2020.

9. Values & Citizenship

Emphasis on ethics, constitutional values, environmental awareness, and social responsibility.

Our Commitment

At GM Legacy School, NEP 2020 is not treated as a policy document alone, but as a living framework that guides our curriculum design, teaching practices, assessment methods, and school culture.

Our commitment is to provide an education that is future-ready, inclusive, skill-oriented, and rooted in Indian values, preparing our students to succeed in higher education, careers, and life.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
