Daily Schedule Overview

GM Legacy School follows a structured daily schedule designed to provide comprehensive education while ensuring adequate time for academic learning, physical activities, and personal development. Our schedule is carefully planned to optimize learning outcomes and student well-being.

School operates Monday through Saturday with different timings for weekdays and Saturday.

Important Guidelines

Please ensure students follow the school timing schedule for optimal learning experience.

Key Points to Remember:

  • Arrival Time: Students should arrive 15 minutes before the first period begins
  • Assembly: Daily assembly is mandatory for all students and includes prayers, announcements, and motivational talks
  • Lunch: Nutritious lunch is provided in the school cafeteria during the designated lunch period
  • Sports Period: Includes various physical activities, games, and fitness exercises
  • Saturday Schedule: Shorter schedule with focus on co-curricular activities and MPT (Moral, Physical Training)

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
