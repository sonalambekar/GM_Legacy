Academic Calendar – GM Legacy School

The Academic Calendar of GM Legacy School is designed to ensure a balanced distribution of teaching, learning, assessment, co-curricular activities, and holidays, in accordance with CBSE guidelines, NEP 2020, and the academic practices of the Government of Karnataka.

The academic session normally runs from June to March.

Tentative Academic Year Plan

Month Major Academic Activities
June Reopening of School, Student Orientation, Commencement of Classes
July Regular Teaching, Unit Tests, Club Activities Begin
August Independence Day Celebrations, Formative Assessments
September Mid-Term Examinations, Teacher-Parent Meetings
October Dasara Holidays, Project Work, Co-curricular Events
November Post Mid-Term Teaching, Sports Meet, Cultural Activities
December Formative Assessments, Annual Day Celebrations, Winter Break
January Revision Classes, Pre-Board Examinations (for Classes X & XII)
February Annual Examinations (Classes I–IX & XI)
March Final Board Examinations, Result Preparation, Session Closure
April–May Summer Vacation, Teacher Training & Planning

Key Components of the Academic Calendar

Teaching–Learning Periods

Two main terms:

Term I

June – September

Term II

October – March

Assessments & Examinations

  • Unit Tests / Periodic Tests
  • Half-Yearly / Mid-Term Examinations
  • Annual Examinations
  • Pre-Board Examinations for Classes X & XII

Holidays & Vacations

  • Summer Vacation: April – May
  • Dasara Holidays: October
  • Winter Break: December
  • National & State Holidays as per Government list

Parent–Teacher Meetings (PTM)

  • After each major assessment
  • Minimum 3 PTMs in an academic year

Co-curricular & Sports Activities

  • Club activities throughout the year
  • Annual Sports Meet
  • Cultural and literary events
  • Community service programmes

Teacher Development Programmes

  • In-service training as per CBSE & NEP 2020
  • Planning and review meetings during vacations

Important Notes

The Academic Calendar is subject to revision based on CBSE notifications, government orders, or unforeseen circumstances.

Any changes will be notified to parents through the school website, circulars, and notice board.

Separate detailed calendars will be issued for:
Board Classes (X & XII) | Examination Schedules | Holiday Lists
