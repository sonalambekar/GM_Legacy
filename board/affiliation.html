CBSE Affiliation Details – GM Legacy School

Board Name

Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi — a national education board recognised by the Government of India.

Affiliation Status

Provisional affiliation granted / Applied for (details to be updated upon issue)

Affiliation Number

To be issued by CBSE upon approval

Affiliation Validity

Initial affiliation period is usually for three (3) years, extendable as per CBSE affiliation bye-laws.

Classes Covered

Affiliation sought for classes from Foundational Stage (Pre-Nursery / Nursery / LKG / UKG) to Secondary School (Class X), with planned upgradation beyond as per CBSE norms.

CBSE Affiliation Requirements

GM Legacy School is being established and developed to fully comply with the CBSE Affiliation Bye-Laws. Key components for affiliation include:

  • Recognition by the State Government of Karnataka and a valid No Objection Certificate (NOC).
  • Adequate land, buildings, classrooms, playground, and infrastructure as prescribed in the affiliation guidelines.
  • Qualified teaching staff and support personnel as per CBSE norms.
  • Facilities for laboratories, library, hygiene, fire safety, sanitation, and physical education.
  • Systems for student assessment, record-keeping, and academic delivery aligned with CBSE curriculum.

Affiliation Process

GM Legacy School is in the process of fulfilling all documentation, infrastructure, and compliance requirements to secure CBSE affiliation. Once granted, we will update this section with the Affiliation Number, Validity Period, and linked PDF of the CBSE affiliation letter.

Why CBSE Affiliation Matters

Affiliation to CBSE enables GM Legacy School to follow a nationally recognised curriculum, preparing students for CBSE board examinations and ensuring quality, consistency, and credibility in education delivery across India and abroad.

Affiliation In Progress

We are committed to meeting all CBSE standards and will update this page with official affiliation details once received.
