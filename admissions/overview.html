Admission Overview

At GM Legacy School, we welcome young learners into a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child is encouraged to learn with joy, confidence, and curiosity. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, fair, and child-centric, in accordance with CBSE guidelines and the principles of NEP 2020.

Admissions are open for students across all stages:

Foundational Stage
(Early Years / Pre-Primary)

Preparatory Stage
(Primary Classes)

Middle School

Secondary School

We invite parents who share our belief in holistic education, strong values, and academic excellence to become part of the GM Legacy School community.

Guiding Principles of Admission

Admissions are based on eligibility, age criteria, and availability of seats.

The school follows a non-discriminatory admission policy, ensuring equal opportunity to all children.

The process is conducted in a fair, transparent, and systematic manner.

No donation or capitation fee is charged, as per CBSE and government norms.

Stages of Admission

1

Enquiry and Information

Parents may visit the school campus, explore the website, or contact the Admissions Office for details.

2

Application Submission

Duly filled application forms (online or offline) must be submitted along with the required documents.

3

Interaction / Assessment

For Foundational and Primary classes: A simple informal interaction with the child and parents may be conducted.

For higher classes: A basic academic assessment may be held to understand the student’s learning level.

4

Confirmation of Admission

Selected candidates will be informed through email/phone, followed by fee payment and document verification.

5

Orientation and Onboarding

Parents and students will attend an orientation programme before the commencement of the academic session.

Important Information

Schedule: Admissions are usually open from January to June for the session beginning in June.

Capacity: Limited seats are available in each class to maintain optimal student–teacher ratio.

Rights: The school reserves the right to grant or deny admission based on eligibility and availability.
