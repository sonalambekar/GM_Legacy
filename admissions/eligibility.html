Eligibility Criteria

Admission to GM Legacy School is granted strictly in accordance with the age norms prescribed by CBSE and the Government of Karnataka, and in alignment with the NEP 2020 stage-wise structure.

Cut-off Date

The age of the child is calculated as on the cut-off date (1st June of the academic year), unless otherwise notified by the State Government or CBSE.

General Guidelines

  • The age criteria may be revised as per CBSE / State Government notifications from time to time.
  • Birth Certificate issued by a competent authority is mandatory as proof of age.
  • For transfer admissions, the age and class will be verified with reference to the previous school records.
  • Relaxation in age, if any, shall be strictly as per government rules.

School’s Right

  • Verify all documents submitted.
  • Refuse admission in case of false information or non-eligibility.
  • Place a child in a class appropriate to his/her age and academic readiness.
