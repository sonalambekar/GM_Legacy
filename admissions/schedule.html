Admission Schedule

Admissions to GM Legacy School are conducted according to a well-defined schedule to ensure a smooth and timely admission process for parents and students. For the upcoming Academic Year, the tentative admission schedule is as follows:

Admission Calendar

Sl. No. Activity Tentative Dates
1 Issue of Enquiry & Application Forms 1st January – 31st March
2 Submission of Application Forms 1st January – 31st March
3 Interaction / Assessment 15th February – 15th April
4 Declaration of Provisional Selection Within 7 days of Interaction
5 Fee Payment & Document Verification Within 5 working days of Selection
6 Issue of Admission Confirmation Immediately after Fee Payment
7 Orientation Programme for Parents & Students Last Week of May
8 Commencement of Academic Session First Week of June

Class-wise Admission Window

January – April

Preparatory Stage

Class I to III

Middle School

Class IV to VIII (Subject to seat availability)

Secondary School

Guidelines Dependent

Class IX & XI (As per CBSE & State Govt guidelines)

Important Notes

  • The above schedule is tentative and may be revised by the School Management from time to time.
  • Admissions are subject to availability of seats in each class.
  • Late applications may be considered only if vacancies exist.
  • For Class IX and XI, admissions will be strictly as per CBSE regulations.
  • Parents are advised to complete all formalities well before the commencement of the academic session.
