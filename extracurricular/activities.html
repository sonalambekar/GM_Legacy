Co & Extra Curricular Activities

Co-Curricular and Extra-Curricular Activities

At GM Legacy School, education extends beyond the classroom. We believe that co-curricular and extra-curricular activities play a vital role in the holistic development of a child, nurturing talents, character, leadership, creativity, and social skills.

In alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 and the CBSE co-scholastic framework, our activity programme complements academic learning and helps students discover their interests, strengths, and passions.

Objectives of Co-Curricular & Extra-Curricular Activities

Our activity programme aims to:

• Promote all-round development of students

• Identify and nurture individual talents and interests

• Build confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills

• Encourage creativity, innovation, and self-expression

• Develop discipline, perseverance, and sportsmanship

• Foster social responsibility and community engagement

Co-Curricular Activities

Co-curricular activities are closely linked with the academic curriculum and support classroom learning.

Academic Enrichment Activities

• Science, Mathematics, and Language Clubs

• Olympiads and Academic Competitions

• Quizzes, debates, and elocution

• Projects, exhibitions, and model-making

• Reading programmes and literary activities

Skill and Enrichment Programmes

• Coding and Robotics Clubs

• Entrepreneurship and Innovation Clubs

• Environmental and Eco Clubs

• Heritage and Culture Clubs

Extra-Curricular Activities

Extra-curricular activities focus on physical, creative, and cultural development.

Sports and Physical Activities

• Athletics and track events

• Team games: Football, Cricket, Basketball, Volleyball

• Indoor games: Chess, Carrom, Table Tennis

• Yoga, fitness, and martial arts

Arts and Cultural Activities

• Music (Vocal and Instrumental)

• Dance (Classical and Folk)

• Drama and Theatre

• Drawing, Painting, and Craft

Leadership and Personality Development

• Student Council and House System

• Leadership training programmes

• Public speaking and communication skills

• Model United Nations (MUN) and Youth Parliaments

Social and Community Activities

• Community service and social outreach

• Swachh Bharat and environmental drives

• Awareness programmes and campaigns

• National festivals and observance days

Stage-wise Activity Focus

Foundational & Primary Stages

• Fun-based activities, games, music, art, and storytelling

• Development of motor skills, creativity, and social interaction

Middle Stage

• Club activities, sports training, cultural participation

• Development of teamwork, discipline, and leadership

Secondary Stage

• Advanced sports training, cultural excellence

• Leadership roles, community service, career-oriented clubs

Organisation of Activities

• Structured activity periods in the weekly timetable

• House system to promote healthy competition

• Inter-house, inter-school, and district-level competitions

• Annual Sports Day, Cultural Fest, and Talent Shows

Assessment and Recognition

• Continuous assessment of co-scholastic development

• Participation and performance recorded in report cards

• Certificates, awards, and recognitions for achievements

• Encouragement of participation over mere winning

Facilities and Support

Sports Fields and Courts

Well-maintained outdoor and indoor sports facilities for various games and athletics

Activity Rooms and Auditorium

Spacious venues for performances, presentations, and group activities

Music, Dance, and Art Studios

Dedicated creative spaces equipped with instruments and art supplies

Qualified Coaches and Instructors

Experienced professionals to guide and mentor students in their chosen activities

Our Commitment

At GM Legacy School, our co-curricular and extra-curricular programme ensures that every student:

• Discovers and develops hidden talents

• Builds confidence, discipline, and leadership

• Learns teamwork, respect, and sportsmanship

• Grows into a balanced, creative, and socially responsible individual

We believe that true education nurtures not only the mind, but also the body, heart, and spirit.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
