Facilities for Children at GM Legacy School

Facilities for Children at GM Legacy School

At GM Legacy School, we provide a safe, modern, and child-friendly infrastructure that supports academic excellence, physical well-being, creativity, and holistic development. Our facilities are thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of children at every stage of schooling, in accordance with CBSE norms and NEP 2020 principles.

Smart Classrooms

GM Legacy School is equipped with smart classrooms that integrate technology with traditional teaching.

Key Features:

• Interactive digital boards and projectors

• Audio-visual teaching aids and multimedia content

• Access to e-learning resources and simulations

• Improved student engagement and concept clarity

• Support for blended learning and digital literacy

Smart classrooms enable teachers to explain complex concepts visually and make learning interactive, engaging, and effective.

Library & Reading Rooms

The school library is the intellectual heart of the campus, promoting a strong culture of reading and research.

Facilities Include:

• Age-appropriate books for all classes

• Reference books, encyclopaedias, and journals

• Fiction, non-fiction, and children's literature

• Quiet reading rooms for individual study

• Digital resources and e-library facilities (as developed)

Regular library periods and reading programmes help develop reading habits, comprehension, and love for learning.

Science & Computer Laboratories

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped laboratories for:

• Physics

• Chemistry

• Biology

Features:

• Modern apparatus and safety equipment

• Hands-on experiments and practical learning

• Demonstration facilities and lab manuals

• Strict safety protocols and supervision

Computer Laboratories

• Sufficient number of computers with internet access

• Latest educational software and coding platforms

• Digital literacy and programming facilities

• Supervised and safe use of technology

These labs promote experiential learning, scientific temper, and technological competence.

Activity Rooms

Dedicated activity rooms are provided for creative and co-curricular development.

Used For:

• Art and craft activities

• Music and dance practice

• Drama and theatre rehearsals

• Club activities and workshops

These spaces encourage creativity, self-expression, teamwork, and talent development.

Playgrounds & Sports Facilities

Physical education is a core part of schooling at GM Legacy School.

Outdoor Facilities:

• Spacious playgrounds

• Fields for football, cricket, and athletics

• Courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton

Indoor Facilities:

• Indoor games room

• Table tennis, chess, carrom

• Yoga and fitness area

Regular sports periods, professional coaching, and competitions promote fitness, discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Health & Medical Care

The school gives top priority to the health and well-being of students.

Facilities Include:

• Fully equipped First Aid Room

• Trained staff for emergency care

• Tie-up with nearby hospitals and doctors

• Regular health check-ups and screening camps

• Awareness programmes on hygiene, nutrition, and fitness

Immediate medical attention is provided whenever required, and parents are promptly informed.

Transport Facility

GM Legacy School provides a safe and reliable transport system for students.

Key Features:

• School buses covering major routes

• GPS-enabled vehicles (as implemented)

• Trained and verified drivers and attendants

• Strict safety and discipline protocols

• Regular maintenance and monitoring

The transport system ensures safe, comfortable, and punctual travel for students.

Safety & Security

Student safety is our highest priority.

Security Measures Include:

• Controlled entry and exit points

• CCTV surveillance in key areas

• Security guards and campus monitoring

• Visitor management and ID system

• Fire safety equipment and drills

• Disaster management and evacuation plans

The campus is designed to be a secure, protected, and child-safe environment.

Special Needs Support

GM Legacy School is committed to inclusive education and equal opportunity for all learners.

Support Services Include:

• Identification of children with special educational needs

• Individualised learning support and counselling

• Remedial teaching and resource support

• Collaboration with parents and specialists

• Sensitisation of teachers and students

We strive to ensure that every child, regardless of ability, receives the care, respect, and support needed to succeed.

Our Commitment

Through these comprehensive facilities, GM Legacy School ensures that every child:

• Learns in a safe, modern, and supportive environment

• Has access to quality infrastructure and resources

• Grows in knowledge, health, confidence, and character

We believe that a well-designed campus is the foundation for effective learning and happy childhood.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
