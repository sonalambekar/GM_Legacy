Residential Facilities at GM Legacy School

Residential Facilities at GM Legacy School

At GM Legacy School, residential facilities are provided to support students from Class III (after completion of 2nd Standard) onwards, offering a safe, caring, and disciplined boarding environment that complements academic learning and holistic development.

Our hostels are designed to create a home away from home, where students grow with independence, responsibility, and strong values under proper guidance and supervision.

Hostel Overview

The school provides separate, well-managed hostels for boys and girls, located within or near the secure school campus.

Key Features:

• Residential facility available from Class III onwards

• Separate hostels for boys and girls

• Comfortable dormitories and study areas

• Structured daily routine integrating study, sports, and rest

• Supervised living with trained residential staff

• Focus on discipline, hygiene, and well-being

The hostel environment promotes academic focus, self-reliance, and balanced development.

Hostel Facilities

Boys Hostel

The Boys Hostel provides a safe, disciplined, and supportive environment for male students.

Facilities Include:

• Well-ventilated dormitories / rooms

• Individual beds, cupboards, and study tables

• Common study halls and recreation areas

• Adequate washrooms and bathing facilities

• Round-the-clock supervision by wardens

Boys are guided to develop discipline, teamwork, leadership, and responsibility.

Girls Hostel

The Girls Hostel offers a secure, caring, and homely atmosphere for female students.

Facilities Include:

• Separate, well-secured hostel building

• Comfortable accommodation and hygienic facilities

• Dedicated lady wardens and caretakers

• Recreation and indoor activity areas

• Strict access control and safety protocols

Special care is taken to ensure the comfort, dignity, and safety of girl students.

Boarding Life

Boarding life at GM Legacy School is structured to ensure a balanced daily routine.

A typical day includes:

Morning routine and physical activity
School hours and academic classes
Supervised study hours (prep time)
Games, sports, and recreation
Evening activities and relaxation
Dinner and night routine

Boarding life helps students develop:

• Independence and self-discipline

• Time management and study habits

• Social skills and teamwork

• Respect for rules and others

Dining & Nutrition

The school provides a nutritious, balanced, and hygienic भोजन व्यवस्था for all boarders.

Dining Facilities Include:

• Spacious and clean dining hall

• Freshly prepared vegetarian meals

• Menu planned by nutrition experts

• Clean drinking water and safe food handling

• Special care for younger children

The diet ensures:

• Proper nutrition for growing children

• Balanced intake of proteins, vitamins, and minerals

• Regular meal timings and discipline

Medical & Wellness

The health and well-being of boarders is a top priority.

Facilities Include:

• In-house First Aid Room in the hostel

• Trained staff for emergency care

• Regular medical check-ups

• Tie-up with nearby hospitals and doctors

• Counselling and emotional support

Parents are informed immediately in case of illness or emergency.

Hostel Rules & Discipline

All boarders are required to follow a clear Hostel Code of Conduct.

Key Rules:

  • Strict adherence to daily routine and timings
  • Mandatory attendance during study hours and meals
  • Maintenance of cleanliness and personal hygiene
  • Respect towards staff and fellow students
  • Prohibition of prohibited items and misconduct

Discipline is maintained through a positive and corrective approach, not punishment.

Safety & Supervision

Safety of residential students is ensured through:

Separate Hostels

Boys and girls accommodation

Controlled Access

Entry and exit system

CCTV Surveillance

Common areas monitoring

Regular monitoring

Fire Safety

Equipment and drills

The hostel campus is maintained as a secure and child-safe zone.

Residential Staff

The hostel is managed by a dedicated team of trained and caring residential staff.

Chief Warden

Boys' Warden

Girls' Warden

Assistant Wardens

Caretakers & Housekeeping

Medical Attendant

Residential staff are:

• Experienced and verified

• Trained in child care and safety

• Available round-the-clock

• In constant coordination with parents and school authorities

Eligibility for Residential Facility

• Residential facility is available only for students from Class III onwards.

• Admission to hostel is subject to availability of seats and approval by the Hostel Committee.

• Separate hostel admission form and rules apply.

Our Commitment

At GM Legacy School, our residential facilities ensure that every boarder:

• Lives in a safe, disciplined, and caring environment

• Develops independence, responsibility, and confidence

• Receives academic support and personal guidance

• Feels at home while growing into a responsible individual

We strive to make boarding life a happy, healthy, and enriching experience for every child.

GM Legacy School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
